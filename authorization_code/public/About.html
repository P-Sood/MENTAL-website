About MENTAL






We are two brothers creating this game out of a bedroom, and after a year of vigorous development the core mechanics of MENTAL is complete and we had to let the world know about it ASAP.
We are university majors in business that have a lot of fun playing games and thought why not make a game that we would love for us and the community to play with.

full release will have the following upgrades:

  • Online Multiplayer (both PVP and Co-op)
  • Complete overhaul of graphics
  • New game mechanics
  • More maps, game modes, and in-game content
  • Improved AI
  • Improved interaction system
  • Improved settings options
  • Character customization
  • Weapon and gun customization
  • Better optimization”

The current features of MENTAL include, but are not limited to:
  • 20+ fully interactive guns
  • Gun attachments
  • Realistic bullet ballistics (bullet penetration, ricochets, refraction, bullet drop and travel time, etc.)
  • 30+ melee weapons
  • Player and gun upgrades
  • Crafting
  • 3 levels of motion (walking, jogging, running)
  • Wallrunning
  • Climbing
  • Jumping & dashing
  • Physics based combat
  • Slicing enemies
  • Destructible environments
  • Interactive and destructible objects
  • Power-Ups
  • Shooter&fighter enemies
  • Fully comprehensive tutorial
  • Realistic gore
  • Safe Mode (turn off gore, drugs, etc.)”
