FAQ


Here are some frequently asked Questions:

Q. How do I craft?
A. Get two weapons and throw them into the pack-a-punch machine

Q. Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?
A. 6-10 months

Q. How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?
A. We will constantly keep updating mental, from community feedback and look for ways to improve the game.

Q. What is the current state of the Early Access version?
A. MENTAL has its core gameplay elements like the movement, shooting and combat completed and everything runs very smoothly most of the time. That being said, this is an Early Access title and you may run into bugs and glitches occasionally. This is a working progress and we will better the game with each update.

Q. Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?
A. We plan to gradually raise the price as we implement new content and features.

Q. How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?
A. We will have discussion boards on Steam, reddit, and discord where users can post questions, complaints, concerns, and feedback. We will also be available to be reached through our website and social media, as well as email. We encourage and accept all feedback, as we want to involve the community and their feedback heavily in our development process.
